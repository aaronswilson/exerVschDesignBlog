Working with a Lathe

November 4, 2021

Working with a lathe can be very helpful when carving round symmetrical objects such as rings, pens, bowls, bats, and many more! It works by rotating the wood piece at various speeds up to 1000 RPM. At these speeds it is possible to carve away at the wood using a chisel like tool designed to chip away at the wood.

Janka Scale

November 9, 2021

One thing that many people take for granted is the importance of selecting the right piece of wood for any particular project. Important factors to take into account include: color, grain, stainability, and wood density or hardness. Of these characteristics, the hardness of a piece of wood is most often taken for granted. The Janka scale is a measurement of how much force is required to push a steel ball into a piece of wood to a desired depth. This information can be used to estimate how difficult it might be to cut or sand various types of wood.
